Jane Doe's Housing Preferences



Affordability



Jane Doe, a diligent college student, is in search of an apartment that perfectly aligns with her budget of $1,300 per month. With her academic pursuits taking center stage, Jane understands the importance of financial responsibility. She seeks an affordable housing option that won't strain her resources, allowing her to comfortably cover rent, utilities, and other living expenses while focusing on her studies.


Roommates



Jane is open to the idea of shared living arrangements. She's looking for an apartment with multiple bedrooms to share with roommates, which not only fosters a sense of camaraderie but also helps in splitting the rent and utility costs.


Amenities



While Jane values affordability, she appreciates apartments that offer desirable amenities within her budget. Laundry facilities, a fitness center, or common lounge areas are bonuses she's open to.
