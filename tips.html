Essential Tips for New Homeowners

Tip #1: Find a realiable handyman

One thing to keep in mind for you and for your house is to find a realiable and trustworthy handyman that will help with home projects. Finding a good handyman, should possess capabilities to deal with a number of home service needed. From hanging artwork to installing light fixtures, having someone to assist with services is necessary. To find a handyman and other services available near you, check out some of the websites listed below to get started: - Angie's list - HomeAdvisor - Nextdoor.com

Tip #2: Get to know your neighbors

Meeting your next-door neighbors is one of the most essential things you can do when moving into a new home. In case of emergency and for creaating a positive community, establishing good rapport with neibors go a long way.

Tip #3: Keep all home warranties and manuals in a safe place

As a new homeowner, you're going to be given a number of appliance manuals and warranties. Make sure you keep all these files organized folder, so you'll be able to find them when its necessary. In addition, you'll need to pass on any new and existing warranty information and manuals to the new homewoners, if and when you sell the house or any other court documentations in case of potential issues.
